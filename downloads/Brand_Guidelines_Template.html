2. Logo & Mark

Primary Logo

MD

Primary Logo (with clear space)

Clear Space & Sizing

Always maintain clear space around the logo. Never allow other elements to crowd the logo or interfere with its legibility.

✓ DO
  • Maintain minimum clear space
  • Use full-color versions
  • Ensure sufficient size (minimum 40px)
✗ DON'T
  • Rotate or distort the logo
  • Add effects or shadows
  • Use colors other than approved palettes

6. Tone of Voice

Your brand voice should be consistent across all communications. Here's how we communicate:

  • Professional yet approachable — Knowledgeable but friendly
  • Clear and concise — Direct communication without jargon
  • Empowering — Focus on customer success and capabilities
  • Authentic — Honest and transparent in all communications

7. Brand Applications

Apply these guidelines consistently across all touchpoints:

  • Website — Main colors, typography, imagery style
  • Social Media — Consistent logo placement, color usage
  • Marketing Materials — Templates with approved colors and fonts
  • Email — Header templates with logo and brand colors
  • Business Cards — Logo, brand colors, consistent typography
  • Presentations — Slide templates using brand colors and fonts
